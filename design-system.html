01 · Principles

Calm is a feature.

Our interface should feel like the studio itself — warm, unhurried, grounded. Four rules we return to when anything feels off.

Warm, not beige

Earth tones with quiet chroma. Never muddy. Never Instagram-filter. Color should feel like morning light on wood.

Measured, not loud

Generous whitespace. One headline per screen. Monospace for metadata. Nothing shouts — the content carries.

Serif holds weight

Display serif for moments of feeling. Sans-serif for getting things done. Mono for facts. Everything in its place.

Human, not corporate

Copy sounds like a coach talking, not a brand. Specific verbs, first names, honest pricing. No wellness-washing.
