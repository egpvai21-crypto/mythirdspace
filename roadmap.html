01
MVP — Foundations & PT pilot
Weeks 0–6
Target: 10–15 PT clients
Success: daily active use by 80% of pilot

Everything a PT coach needs to run their book of clients end-to-end: onboard, assess, program, book, and keep notes. No AI summaries yet, no group features, no gamification. The bar is "replaces the coach's notebook, spreadsheet and WhatsApp thread."

M-01 Ops · Platform

PWA shell & auth

Installable web app with email / phone OTP login, role-based routing (client vs. coach vs. admin), and offline-safe shell.

  • Add-to-home-screen prompt
  • Session persistence across devices
  • Role guard on every route
Depends onfoundation
M-02 Client portal

Client onboarding form

Structured intake capturing MSK & movement assessment, injuries, health history, short- & long-term goals. Saves as the client's living profile.

  • Multi-step form with autosave
  • Coach-visible, client-editable
  • Re-assessable at any cadence
Depends onM-01
M-03 Client portal

Body composition log

Manual input for weight, body fat %, muscle mass, measurements. Timeline view for the client, latest-snapshot view for the coach.

  • Free-form entry fields
  • Simple line chart per metric
  • Attach a photo per entry
Depends onM-02
M-04 Client portal

Health records vault

Uploads for blood work, scans, medical notes. PDF / image, coach-permissioned, with a visible "shared with" audit line.

  • PDF + image upload
  • Per-file coach access toggle
Depends onM-02
M-05 Coach universe

Coach profile & calendar

Public trainer profile (photo, bio, specialisms, intro video) + personal calendar with day / week / month views. Peer calendars are read-only.

  • Profile editor
  • Own bookings editable, peers read-only
  • Block-out time & off-days
Depends onM-01
M-06 Client portal

PT session booking

Book by trainer or by time slot. Coach-approval window of 5–15 min before a slot auto-confirms; cancel / reschedule within policy window.

  • Book-by-coach & book-by-time flows
  • 12h advance booking default
  • Client sees pending / confirmed state
Depends onM-05M-12
M-07 Coach universe

Session notes & programming

Per-session notes attached to the client profile. Exercise log with sets / reps / load, visible historically so any coach can pick up where the last one left off.

  • Quick-log template per session
  • Coach-to-coach handoff view
  • Client sees a read-only summary
Depends onM-02M-06
M-08 Client portal

PB tracker

Personal bests for compound lifts (squat, bench, deadlift, OHP) plus custom movements — mobility milestones, first pull-up, etc.

  • Pre-seeded compound list
  • Custom goals with target date
Depends onM-02
M-09 Policy

Booking & cancellation rules

System-enforced policy layer: 12h advance window, same-day cutoffs, no-show handling. Rules live in config so admins can adjust once.

  • Config-driven windows
  • No-show auto-flagging
  • Override requires admin
Depends onM-06
M-10 Coach universe

Time-off & block requests

Coaches request off-days or hour blocks; admin approves. Clients with standing sessions are auto-notified with alternate-coach options.

  • Request flow + admin approval
  • Client-side impact notification
Depends onM-05
M-11 Ops · Platform

Admin console (v1)

Minimal back office: manage coaches, approve time-off, edit policy config, view client roster. Not pretty, just functional.

Depends onM-01
M-12 Ops · Platform

Notifications — core

Push + email for booking confirmed / declined, session reminder (24h & 2h), coach off-day impact, policy breaches. One plumbing, many senders.

02
Beta — Intelligence & nutrition
Weeks 6–14
Scale: all PT + first GX cohort
Success: AI summary usage + Kenko opt-in > 40%

The pilot has proved the plumbing works. Beta adds the things that make the product distinctive: AI-assisted profiling, wearable / BCA integrations, weekly nutrition planning with Kenko, and a slice of group-class tracking. Gamification arrives — quietly — as personal badges, not leaderboards.

Risk — behavioural

Gamification done quietly

Leaderboards can demotivate as much as motivate. Default to private streaks (B-07); only unlock opt-in public leaderboards (L-06) once the culture is established.

Risk — policy

No manual exceptions

Once booking / trial / cancellation rules ship (M-09, B-11), admins should not routinely override. Every bypass is future entitlement.

Risk — data

AI needs clean inputs

B-01 and L-02 only pay off if onboarding (M-02) and body comp (M-03) are structured, not free text. Invest in the schema before the model.
